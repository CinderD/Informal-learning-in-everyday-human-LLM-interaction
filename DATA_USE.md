# Data Use Notes

This repository releases derived, de-identified analytic artifacts for verifying the manuscript results. It does not redistribute raw public conversation text or original conversation identifiers.

Users who want to reproduce the full pipeline from raw conversations should obtain WildChat, LMSYS Chat-1M and ShareChat from the original providers and comply with those datasets' licences, terms of use and privacy restrictions.

The derived label tables in this repository are intended for scholarly verification, reanalysis and extension of the aggregate results. They should not be used to attempt re-identification, reconstruction of raw conversations or linkage to original user histories.
